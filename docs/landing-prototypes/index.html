SSANDMAN

Landing page prototypes.

Twelve directions for presenting Sandman: from early control-plane concepts to the CLI-owned delivery candidate that landed as the live landing page, plus three session notes — two of which have been promoted into the canonical POSITIONING doc. Each card is annotated with its current status (Shipped, Canonical, Merged into POSITIONING, or Superseded) so future contributors can tell which direction is the live one.
